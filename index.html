Our Story

In the vibrant heart of Santurce, San Juan, Ombra Italian Eatery stands as a testament to authentic Italian culinary excellence.

Led by renowned Michelin-starred Chef Alfredo Esposito, whose family's culinary legacy spans over 130 years in Abruzzo, Italy, we bring you an intimate dining experience that marries tradition with innovation.

From his native Italy to Venezuela, and now to Puerto Rico, Chef Esposito's journey has been one of passion, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to the art of Italian cuisine.

"Ombra" — meaning shadow in Italian — reflects our philosophy: subtle elegance, depth of flavor, and the interplay of light and darkness in every dish.
