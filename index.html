Our Story

A journey spanning generations, continents, and countless unforgettable meals

Roots in Abruzzo

Chef Alfredo Esposito's culinary journey began in the rolling hills of Abruzzo, Italy, where his family has been crafting authentic Italian cuisine for over 130 years. The traditions, recipes, and passion passed down through generations form the foundation of every dish at Ombra.

Michelin Recognition

Chef Esposito's dedication to excellence earned him the prestigious Michelin star, a testament to his innovative approach while honoring traditional Italian cooking. His journey from Italy to Venezuela and finally to Puerto Rico has enriched his culinary perspective.

Ombra Today

Since opening in 2020, Ombra has become a culinary destination in San Juan's vibrant Santurce neighborhood. Our recently renovated space features sleek, modern design that perfectly complements the sophisticated yet warm dining experience. Recognized as a top 10 restaurant in Puerto Rico, we continue to redefine high-end Italian cuisine in the Caribbean.
